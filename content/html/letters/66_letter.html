July 16.

Many thanks for the information about prices. I have
accepted an invitation in the country for July 26-
8, but probably you will have had enough by the
26th. I am in the throes of the coinage, and doubt
whether I shall have finished by the 22nd but will try.
Meanwhile I will expect you on the 22nd

Yours ever
B.P.G.
