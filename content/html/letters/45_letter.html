Queen's Coll. April 26.
I am just back. Can you let me have
your remarks on the the proofs soon? All our
Papyri (21 packing cases!) are on their way
to Oxford. The Hibeh things ought to be
very nice. I hope P.P.III is nearing the end.

Yours
B.PG.
