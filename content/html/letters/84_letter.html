Queen's College
Oxford
May 2
My dear Smyly,
The enclosed was sent
me by a German who did not know
who you were. I sincerely trust
his suggestions came too late!
We are struggling with the
Hibeh literary pieces which are
in many cases very difficult to
decipher especially in this lad
