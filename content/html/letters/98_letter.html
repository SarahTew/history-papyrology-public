[begin page 2]


Delighted to hear you will come in July. We
shall be both here after the 4th indefinitely. I havent
nearly finished App. i. The result of going over the
καταφύλλον reports with the other things in my head is
to bring out several new points and the commentary on 62b
will be frightfully hashed up again. We have also
fished up on a verso some more decrees of Euerg II
rather like 5, but that d-l to read. These too have
to be printed. As to lend ἐν επιστάσει I think I have
got a theory which will work. It is a misidentification of yours.
App. ii on the coins wont get started for another 2 or 3

weeks.
Yours ever
B.P.G.
