Queen's College
Oxford
June 4

My dear Smyly,
Many thanks for your kind
invitation. There is nothing I should
like better than prolonging my stay
in your hospitable city, but my visit
must be very brief this year and Hunt
does not propose to come^.(he wants to go to his people) We both
look forward to coming next year
and staying some [litlte?] time, bringing
some papyri with us. But amid
the appelling distractions of the summer
