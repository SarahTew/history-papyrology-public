The reference to ὅταν ἡ ἄφεσις is Pap.
Amherst Part II. no 43. 9. the previous words
are ἀποδότω δὲ...ἐν μηνὶ Αὐδυναίωι
Αἰγυπτίων δὲ Ἐπεὶφ. By all means quote it.
We leave on the 20th. You should get the Fayûm
vol. in the week after next: We have done a good
deal of the 2nd Amherst Vol. The Treasurer of the E.EF.
is a little fractious about the arrangement with the
Americans abo concerning the crocodile volume, but I hope
to manage things all right. A consignment of 19 Oxyrhynchus
papyri will reach Trin Coll. in a few days.

Yours B.P.G.
