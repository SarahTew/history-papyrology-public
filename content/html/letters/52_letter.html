Nov 19.

Hibeh I goes to Press tomorrow & I leave
in the afternoon. I am sending J.P.M. a
proof of the whole vol, and you will receive
proof of the it all except the introd. and
literary pieces. Have you got your ticket?
We shall leave Cairo for Benimazar on
about Dec. 4th. Keep an eye open for J.G.
Milne in Egypt. He is starting on the 29th & will
probably be at the Hotel du Nil.1

Au revoir
Yours ever B.P.G.
