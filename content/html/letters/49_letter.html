[begin page 2]


A box containing Tebtunis mummy
cartonnage has been despatched to you & will I
hope arrive safely. Dont let M. have more to do
with the unrolling than you can help. I leave
on Monday aft. Many thanks for your letter
on the metrol. fragment,1 I have been so busy the last
few days in delivering lectures etc, that I have not
had time to study it properly, but will utilise it
before I leave. Proof will be sent you in batches
as before. We shall return before the end of March
as the vol. must be out next June. So we should like the
proof back by March 20

Goodbye. Yours ever BPG.
